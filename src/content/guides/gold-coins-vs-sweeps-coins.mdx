The fastest way to understand a sweepstakes casino: **Gold Coins are for fun, Sweeps Coins
are for prizes.** Confusing the two is the most common — and most expensive — beginner
mistake. Here's the full comparison and the traps to avoid.

## Gold Coins, briefly

A **play-only** currency with **no monetary value**. Use them for entertainment; buy more if
you want extended play. They can never be redeemed — when they run low, most casinos top you
up free daily.

## Sweeps Coins, briefly

The **prize** currency. Accumulated redeemable Sweeps Coins can be exchanged for cash or gift
cards under each operator's rules after KYC. You never buy them directly — they arrive free
with Gold Coin purchases and through no-purchase methods. Full detail:
[Sweeps Coins explained](/guides/sweeps-coins-explained/).

## The two mistakes that cost players money

### Mistake 1 — expecting to convert Gold Coins into Sweeps Coins

You can't — they're separate wallets. Most casinos let you **switch between Gold Coin and
Sweeps Coin play modes** with a toggle, but that is *not* a conversion: the balances never
transfer into each other. A Gold Coin package may *include* bonus SC, but the Gold Coins
themselves never become redeemable. Buying Gold Coins to "cash out later" is throwing money
away — and, for the record, **you cannot win real money with Gold Coins**; only Sweeps Coins
redeem.

### Mistake 2 — ignoring the playthrough

Sweeps Coins from a bonus usually must be played through **once (1×)** before they're
redeemable, and every redemption has a **minimum**. The real value of an offer is the SC
left *after* those two conditions — not the headline number.

## Which currency should you actually care about?

The **Sweeps Coins** — worth roughly **$1 each** in redemption value. Because Gold Coins
can't be redeemed, the entire payout value of any welcome offer or promotion lives in its SC,
after playthrough and the minimum. When you compare brands, look past the giant Gold Coin

## When buying Gold Coins isn't worth it

Purchases are always optional — and a poor idea if:

- **You're playing for value.** Gold Coins hold none; only the bundled bonus SC does.
- **You're in a restricted state** where SC can't be redeemed anyway. Check the
  [state legality hub](/state-legality/).
- **You haven't used the free path.** Daily bonuses, social drops, and mail-in requests get
  you Sweeps Coins without spending a cent.
