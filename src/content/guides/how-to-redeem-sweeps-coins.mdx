Redeeming [Sweeps Coins](/guides/sweeps-coins-explained/) for cash or a gift card comes down
to three prerequisites and five steps. Get the prerequisites right the first time and most
redemptions are routine.

## The three prerequisites

Before a payout is even possible you need all three:

1. **Enough redeemable SC** — typically **50 SC** (gift cards) to **100 SC** (cash),
   depending on the operator.
2. **Any playthrough cleared** — Sweeps Coins from a *bonus* usually need to be played
   through **once (1×)**; SC won from gameplay is normally redeemable directly. (See
   [bonus vs redeemable SC](/guides/sweeps-coins-explained/).)
3. **Completed identity verification (KYC)** — a government ID, sometimes a selfie and
   proof of address.

## The five steps

1. **Build a redeemable balance** through gameplay until you're above the minimum.
2. **Verify identity early** — submit KYC as soon as you register, *before* your first
   redemption request. This is the biggest single delay-avoider.
3. **Open the cashier** and choose **Redeem** (not Buy).
4. **Enter the SC amount** and pick a payout method.
5. **Submit and track** the request through the operator's review.

## Payout methods and typical speed

Speed depends on the method and the operator. As a rule of thumb:

| Method | Typical relative speed | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| E-wallet (Skrill / PayPal) | Fastest | Often the lowest-friction option |
| Gift card | Fast | Frequently the lowest minimum |
| Crypto | Fast | Offered by some operators only |
| Bank / ACH transfer | Slower | Usually a few business days |

## Why redemptions stall — and how to avoid it

Almost every delay traces to one of these:

- **KYC pending** — verify before you request, not after.
- **Proof-of-address rejected** — use a bank-statement PDF (not a screenshot); make sure
  your ID matches your registered address.
- **First-time or large redemption** — can trigger a manual review.
- **Weekend / holiday** — processing windows pause.

## When you *won't* be able to redeem

Set expectations up front — redemption isn't possible if:

- You're in a **banned or restricted state** where prizes can't be redeemed (Idaho, for
  example, prohibits cash redemption). Check the [state legality hub](/state-legality/).
- You **haven't cleared the playthrough** on bonus SC yet.
- You **decline KYC** — no verification, no payout at a legitimate operator.
- Your balance is **below the minimum**, or made up of Gold Coins (which are never
  redeemable).
