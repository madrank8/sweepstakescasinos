**Sweeps Coins (SC)** are the currency that gives a sweepstakes casino its value: unlike
[Gold Coins](/guides/gold-coins-vs-sweeps-coins/), which are play-only, Sweeps Coins can be
**redeemed for prizes**. This guide explains what they are, the important difference
between *bonus* and *redeemable* SC, and every free way to collect them.

## What Sweeps Coins actually are

A Sweeps Coin represents a **sweepstakes entry**. It has **no cash value while it sits in
your account** — but redeemable Sweeps Coins can be exchanged for cash or gift cards under
each operator's official rules once you meet the minimum threshold and pass identity
verification (KYC). This promotional structure, with a mandatory free entry method, is what
keeps the model legal in most states. New to all of this? Read
[what is a sweepstakes casino](/guides/what-are-sweepstakes-casinos/) first.

## Bonus SC vs redeemable SC (the key distinction)

Your balance is usually split in two:

- **Bonus SC** — Sweeps Coins received from a promotion. These typically must be **played
  through once (1x)** before they become eligible to redeem.
- **Redeemable SC** — Sweeps Coins won from gameplay. These can be cashed out once you hit
  the operator's minimum (commonly 50–100 SC).

Good operators show both balances clearly in the cashier. A low or transparent playthrough
requirement is one of the things our [rating methodology](/how-we-rate/) rewards.

## Every free (no-purchase) way to get Sweeps Coins

The law requires a free path, and the best operators make it generous:

- **Daily login bonus** — the most reliable steady drip; log in every day for the streak.
- **Social-media giveaways** — follow each brand for weekly SC drops and codes.
- **Email / SMS promos** — opt in for flash offers and reload SC.
- **Referral rewards** — invite friends for bonus SC on both sides.
- **Mail-in request (AMOE)** — the classic sweepstakes method: a handwritten postal request,
  following the exact format in the operator's rules, earns free SC with no purchase.
- **Free bonus with Gold Coin purchases** — optional; buying a GC package includes bonus SC.
