A [Sweeps Coin](/guides/gold-coins-vs-sweeps-coins/) is the only currency at a sweepstakes
casino that carries real value — it's what you eventually redeem for prizes. This guide
covers what it actually represents, the bonus-vs-redeemable split that confuses newcomers,
and the realistic free ways to collect it.

## What a Sweeps Coin represents

Think of a Sweeps Coin as a **sweepstakes entry**, not a chip. It has **no cash value while
it sits in your balance** — but redeemable SC can be exchanged for cash or gift cards under
each operator's official rules once you clear the minimum and pass identity verification.
That structure, anchored by a mandatory free entry method, is what keeps the model legal in
most states (more in [are sweepstakes casinos legit](/guides/are-sweepstakes-casinos-legit/)).

> **Not the same as a "SWEEP" crypto token.** Sweeps Coins are an operator's in-app
> promotional currency, not a tradable cryptocurrency and not the same as generic "SC" tokens
> you'll find on exchanges. They exist only inside the sweepstakes casino you earned them at.

## What is a Sweeps Coin worth?

**One Sweeps Coin is worth about $1 USD in redemption value** at most operators — so 100
redeemable SC is roughly a $100 prize. Two caveats matter:

- **No value until redeemed.** SC has zero cash value while it sits in your account; it only
  becomes money (or a gift card) after a successful redemption.
- **Minimums apply.** You usually need **50–100 SC** to redeem, and **bonus** SC must clear
  its playthrough first (below). So a headline "free 2 SC" is ~$2 of *eventual* value, not
  cash you can withdraw immediately.

## Bonus SC vs redeemable SC

Your balance is usually split in two, and only one half can be cashed out:

- **Bonus SC** — received from a promotion; typically must be **played through once (1×)**
  before it becomes eligible to redeem.
- **Redeemable SC** — won from gameplay; can be cashed out once you reach the minimum
  (commonly 50–100 SC).

A low, clearly-stated playthrough is a quality signal our [rating methodology](/how-we-rate/)
rewards.

## Who shouldn't bother chasing Sweeps Coins

Be honest with yourself — grinding free SC isn't worth it if:

- **You'd have to play in a restricted state** where redemption is blocked. Check the
  [state legality hub](/state-legality/) first.
- **You value your time highly** — daily free SC is real but small; treat it as a bonus, not
  a wage.
- **You won't complete KYC** — unredeemable SC is just entertainment currency.
