## What New Jersey law says about sweepstakes casinos

New Jersey licenses internet gaming under the **Casino Control Act**, administered by the **Division of Gaming Enforcement (DGE)**, with unlicensed gambling addressed under the state's criminal code. The DGE has consistently taken the position that online products offering prizes for chance-based play, without New Jersey authorization, fall outside the regulated market — and it has pressured such operators accordingly.

In 2025, New Jersey lawmakers **advanced dedicated legislation** targeting online sweepstakes casinos, reinforcing the state's restrictive stance and its emphasis on protecting licensed operators and consumers.
