## What New York law says about sweepstakes casinos

**Senate Bill 5935-A** adds Penal Law sections criminalizing online sweepstakes casino games that employ two virtual currencies — one for play and one redeemable for prizes or cash equivalents. The statute explicitly reaches **promoters and marketing affiliates**, which is why Sweepstakes Wiz enforces site-wide affiliate suppression for New York.

Governor Hochul signed the bill **December 5, 2025**; it took effect immediately. The New York Attorney General had already sent cease-and-desist letters to 26 operators in June 2025, presaging statutory action.

> **Direct answer:** New York banned dual-currency online sweepstakes casinos effective December 5, 2025, with affiliate liability. We publish informational content only for New York visitors and do not promote operator bonus offers.
