RPG-STYLE CARD BATTLES — HOW YOU EARN MYSTERY COINS
At the heart of Card Crush is a collectible card game. You unlock cards by opening Mystery Boxes, build a deck, and battle other players in five-round matches — each round the higher Battle Score wins, and you need to take 3 of 5 rounds to win the battle.
