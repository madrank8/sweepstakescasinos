🪙 How American Luck Works: Gold Coins vs Sweeps Coins

American Luck uses the standard US sweepstakes dual-currency model. Gold Coins (GC) are the free, for-fun currency — they have no cash value and cannot be redeemed. Sweeps Coins (SC) are a separate promotional currency you receive free (or as a bonus with optional Gold Coin purchases); Sweeps Coins won through gameplay can be redeemed for cash prizes once you meet the rules. This structure is what lets the platform operate as a sweepstakes rather than real-money gambling. New to the model? See our guides on what a sweepstakes casino is and Gold Coins vs Sweeps Coins.
