## Design Context

### Users
Adults researching U.S. sweepstakes casinos who want to decide whether a platform is eligible, understandable, and worth trying. They need fast access to current restrictions, offer conditions, redemption details, and evidence showing what the reviewer actually experienced.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, candid, and energetic. SweepstakesWiz should feel like an independent specialist publication: confident enough to make a judgment, careful enough to label uncertainty, and approachable enough for a first-time player.

### Aesthetic Direction
An editorial evidence ledger within the existing SweepstakesWiz navy, violet, gold, and warm-white visual language. Favor strong typography, numbered session moments, source/date labels, and visibly distinct verified, pending, and not-tested states. Avoid generic affiliate landing-page hype, casino-floor visual noise, and unsupported urgency.

### Design Principles
1. Put eligibility and material limitations before promotional details.
2. Make evidence status unmistakable; never style placeholders as completed testing.
3. Separate firsthand observations, operator claims, and editorial analysis visually.
4. Use asymmetric editorial rhythm rather than a uniform grid of cards.
5. Keep critical content and status labels usable on mobile and with reduced motion.
