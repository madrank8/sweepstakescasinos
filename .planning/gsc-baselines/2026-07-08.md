Volume is low by design (brand-new coverage). Treat this as a **T-0 starting line**, not a trend.

## 5. GSC tool limitations hit
- **Query-level clicks are fully anonymized** at this volume — "top queries by clicks" is unavailable; use page dimension for click attribution.
- **`sort_by` / `sort_direction` are not honored server-side** — rows return alphabetically. Pulled `row_limit: 1000` and sorted client-side (confirmed quirk).
- **`batch_url_inspection` caps at 10 URLs/call** — the 13 priority URLs were split into two batches (10 + 3).
- **28-day and 90-day windows are effectively identical** here (data only since ~Jun 29), so this baseline is a single T-0 snapshot.
