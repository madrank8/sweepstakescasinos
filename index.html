What Makes a Sweepstakes Casino Different from a Regular Online Casino?

The one-line answer: you never have to deposit to play or to win. Traditional online casinos require real-money wagers; sweepstakes casinos operate under US promotional sweepstakes law using two virtual currencies instead. Gold Coins (GC) are for free social play and carry no monetary value. Sweeps Coins (SC) are the promotional currency — and in eligible states, SC you've accumulated can be redeemed for real prizes once you pass identity verification.

What keeps the model legal is the mandatory free entry path. Every legitimate brand must offer a way to obtain SC with no purchase — sign-up gifts, daily logins, social giveaways, and mail-in (AMOE) requests. You're never required to spend money to participate or to cash out. Brands like Zula lean into this with a no-code welcome of up to 120,000 GC plus Sweeps Coins, available in all but a few states.

  • Refer friends: Most sweeps casinos offer SC bonuses when referred friends complete registration and KYC. A small group of active referrals can generate meaningful passive SC income.

How SC Redemptions Work — What to Expect Step by Step

Understanding the redemption process before you start playing prevents the most common frustrations new players encounter. Here is the full sequence at a high-performing sweepstakes casino:

Step 1 — Complete KYC before you need it. Submit your government-issued ID and selfie verification immediately after registration — not when you want to redeem. KYC processing can take 24–72 hours and you cannot request a payout until it is complete.

Step 2 — Meet the playthrough requirement. Most platforms require a 1× playthrough on your SC balance before redemption. This means if you receive 10 SC free, you need to wager 10 SC in promotional mode before those winnings become redeemable. This is standard and player-friendly — some competitors require 3× or higher.

Step 3 — Reach the minimum threshold. Minimum redemption amounts vary between 10 and 100 SC depending on the platform. The best casinos on this list have minimums of 10–20 SC, making your first redemption accessible much sooner.

Step 4 — Choose your payout method. Options include PayPal, Skrill, ACH bank transfer, cryptocurrency, or gift cards. Crypto and e-wallet redemptions typically process fastest. Bank transfers can take 3–5 business days even after approval.

Are the Bonus Values Advertised by Sweepstakes Casinos Accurate?

Mostly accurate on the headline — misleading on the fine print. The advertised number ("500,000 GC + 10 SC") is generally what lands in your account. The Gold Coin figure, however large, has no redemption value at all.
