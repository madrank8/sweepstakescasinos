Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

California CCPA/CPRA opt-out request for SweepstakesWiz.com.

Last updated: __UPDATED_DATE__

Our position

Sweepstakes Wiz does not sell personal information for money. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), some advertising, analytics, or cookie-based sharing may still be treated as a “sale” or “sharing.” California residents may opt out of that activity.

How to opt out

To submit a “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” request:

  1. Use our contact form.
  2. Put CCPA Do Not Sell in the subject line.
  3. Include the email address you use with us (if any) and enough detail for us to locate your records.

We will confirm receipt and process valid requests within the timeframes required by California law. You will not be discriminated against for exercising CCPA/CPRA rights.

Cookies and analytics

You can also limit tracking via browser controls and our Cookie Policy. See the full Privacy Policy for categories of data we collect and your other rights.

Authorized agents

If you are an authorized agent submitting on behalf of a California resident, say so in the message and include proof of authorization plus a way for us to verify the consumer’s identity.
