Cookie Policy

The small number of cookies and similar technologies this site uses, and how to control them.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. We use them sparingly and never to build advertising profiles. This page sits alongside our Privacy Policy, which covers data handling more broadly.

Categories we use

CategoryPurposeCan disable?
Strictly necessaryKeep the site working (e.g. remembering a dismissed banner, basic security).No
AnalyticsAnonymous, aggregated traffic statistics with IP anonymization so we can improve content.Yes
Affiliate trackingSet by the sweepstakes operator on their domain when you click out, so they can credit the referral.Yes

Geolocation

We use coarse, network-level location (country/state) to comply with state-by-state advertising restrictions — see our State Legality hub. This is derived from your IP at the edge and is not a tracking cookie.

How to control cookies

  • Block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
  • Use private/incognito browsing.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics via the official browser add-on.

Blocking analytics or affiliate cookies does not change your bonus eligibility or registration at any operator.

Changes

We update this policy as our tooling changes; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision. Questions? Contact us.
