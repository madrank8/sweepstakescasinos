Accessibility Statement

We want every reader to be able to use this site, regardless of ability.

Last updated: __UPDATED_DATE__

Our commitment

SweepstakesWiz.com aims to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. Accessibility is an ongoing effort and we continually work to improve the experience for all users.

What we do

  • Semantic HTML structure with logical heading order and landmarks.
  • Descriptive alt text on meaningful images and labels on interactive controls.
  • Color choices intended to meet AA contrast on body text and key UI.
  • Keyboard-operable navigation and visible focus states.
  • Responsive layouts that reflow without horizontal scrolling on small screens.

Known limitations

Some third-party or embedded content (for example, operator redirect pages or external media) may not fully meet our standards. Where we identify barriers we cannot directly fix, we work to provide an accessible alternative.

Report a barrier

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please tell us via the contact page with "Accessibility" in the subject. Include the page URL and a description of the issue, and we will respond and prioritize a fix.

Responsible-play resources

Accessibility also means making help easy to reach. Responsible-gaming support is linked in the bar at the top of every page and detailed on our Responsible Gaming page. Need help now? Call 1-800-GAMBLER.
